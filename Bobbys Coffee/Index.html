Hand Crafted, Small Batch Coffee

We are a microroastery based in Bedford. We roast to order in small batches, focusing on craftsmanship and flavour to produce the best cup of coffee possible from our beans sourced from around the world. Join our adventure and discover your perfect cup.

50% Brazil Sweetcup

50% Brazil Fazenda Cachoeira

Medium Roast

Tasting notes: Dark Chocolate, Caramel, Nuts and Cranberries.

This blend is the result of hours of roasting and tasting alongside experienced baristas to create what we believe to be a fantastic all around coffee for the Barista, the professional and home enthusiast.

It’s a versatile blend that tastes great in a shot of espresso, as an americano or a velvety flat white and equally as delicious dripping through a V60, strained through a french press or bubbling through a stove top Moka pot.

It’s bright, balanced and filled with flavours we love at Beleza Coffee Co. and we think you will too.
